Artist

About

Anica Kitanoska (b. 23 January 2000, Vevcani, North Macedonia) is a visual artist working between mosaic and painting.

BA in Visual Arts and Mosaic (2021), Academy of Fine Arts, Ravenna · MA specialization in Mosaic (2024), Academy of Fine Arts, Ravenna.

PNA Winner 2023 DLUM First Prize 2024 Chartres Second Prize 2024 Osten Jury Recognition 2024

Artist Statement

My practice unfolds at the intersection of mosaic and painting, where gesture and material act as equal carriers of meaning. The tessera becomes a petrified brushstroke - a fragment of movement embedded in matter, shaping the surface through rhythm, direction and energy.
The works emerge as hybrid forms that move beyond the traditional boundaries of painting, sculpture and mosaic. Through layered reliefs of color, cement and light, each stratum introduces a new gesture, texture and spatial tension, allowing time and perception to crystallize within the work.
